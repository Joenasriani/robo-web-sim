# Lessons Documentation

## Overview

Three beginner lessons teach fundamental robotics navigation concepts.

## Lesson 1: Move Forward to a Target
Navigate the robot forward to reach the green target.

## Lesson 2: Turn and Reach a Target
Practice turning controls to orient and reach the target.

## Lesson 3: Avoid an Obstacle and Reach a Goal
Navigate around obstacles to reach the target.

## Progress Tracking
Lesson completion is stored in localStorage under the key `robo-web-sim-completed-lessons`.
